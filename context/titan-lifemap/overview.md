# Titan LifeMap™ — Overview

> The Behavioural Discovery Engine. GAIA's first concrete product.

---

## What It Is

Titan LifeMap is a multi-stage, AI-guided discovery conversation that gives consumers clarity on their life and finances before any regulated advice happens. It replaces the traditional fact-find's "what do you own?" opening with "what matters most?" — financial information supports the conversation, it does not lead it.

It is the first buildable expression of [[gaia/overview]] and the concrete answer to the "Develop D2C financial guidance process" project tracked in `gtd/projects.md`.

## The Seven Titans Framework

Titan Mindset (Patrick's trademarked personal operating system) is built around seven archetypes representing roles people play throughout life:

1. **The Visionary** — purpose, dreams, future direction, life design
2. **The Warrior** — discipline, action, resilience, execution
3. **The Scholar** — learning, understanding, informed decision-making
4. **The Steward** — responsible management of resources (this is where financial planning naturally sits)
5. **The Guardian** — protection and risk management
6. **The Builder** — creating assets, opportunities, legacy
7. **The Sage** — wisdom, perspective, fulfilment

## Discovery Sequencing

Titan LifeMap does not ask about pensions, ISAs, or investments first. It moves through five stages in this order:

1. **Visionary** — Life Vision, Definition of Enough
2. **Sage** — Purpose & Legacy, Health-Wealth-Happiness
3. **Builder** — Core Values, Family & Relationships
4. **Steward** — Relationship With Money, hard facts begin
5. **Guardian** — Behavioural Patterns, protection-related hard facts

Only after Visionary through Guardian does financial data gathering happen in earnest. The philosophy: *"People do not need a financial plan first. They need clarity about the life the financial plan is supposed to support."*

Warrior and Scholar are part of the broader Titan Mindset framework but are not separate Discovery stages — their themes (action/execution, learning/understanding) surface within the Momentum Plan output rather than as dedicated capture stages.

## Soft-Fact Categories and Named Outputs

| Category | Captures | Named Output |
|---|---|---|
| A. Life Vision | Ideal future, what success feels like | Future Vision Statement |
| B. Definition of Enough | What "enough" means, financial security markers | Personal Enough Statement |
| C. Core Values | Top values from: Freedom, Security, Family, Adventure, Contribution, Achievement, Independence, Health, Learning, Community | Top Five Values |
| D. Family and Relationships | Dependants, priorities, caring responsibilities, legacy intentions | Family Impact Summary |
| E. Relationship With Money | Money history, biggest success/disappointment, confidence level | Money Story Summary |
| F. Behavioural Patterns | Procrastination, avoidance, overthinking, impulsiveness, delegation, lack of confidence | Behavioural Friction Profile |
| G. Health, Wealth and Happiness | Current satisfaction, desired state, biggest gap | Health-Wealth-Happiness Scorecard |
| H. Purpose and Legacy | Impact desired, family legacy, charitable intentions | Legacy Statement |

## Output Naming

- **Titan LifeMap Profile** — the consumer-facing summary of all soft facts
- **Titan Clarity Score** — composite score reflecting how clear the user's vision and "enough" definition are
- **Titan Momentum Plan** — ranked priority actions bridging current state to stated goals

## The Three Handoff Routes (Client-Visible)

1. **Self-Guided Consumer** — Titan LifeMap Report, Clarity Summary, Priority Actions, educational guidance. No regulated advice.
2. **Sustain Momentum Coaching** — output becomes a coaching baseline, accountability framework, progress measurement tool. Used by Patrick and Avdokia.
3. **IFA Referral** — output becomes an adviser-ready pack: Personal Profile, Financial Snapshot, Planning Priorities, Behavioural Insights.

## The Fourth Output (Never Client-Visible)

Every session, regardless of route, also generates an **Internal AI Profile** — see `context/titan-lifemap/internal-ai-profile.md` (created during implementation) for full detail. In short: *"The client sees the report. GAIA sees the person."* This is the permanent memory layer behind GAIA, not a byproduct.
