# GAIA — Overview

> Patrick's AI-powered financial planning platform in development.

---

## What GAIA Is

GAIA is a developing AI-powered financial planning platform designed to help consumers and advisers navigate a structured financial planning process. The long-term vision is a scalable digital planning ecosystem combining financial planning, behavioural support, and AI.

## Stage

GAIA now has a defined first product: **Titan LifeMap™**, a Behavioural Discovery Engine in active build (see `context/titan-lifemap/overview.md`). It is being built API-first on the existing VPS infrastructure (`api.sustain-momentum.com`), decoupled from the WordPress marketing site so it can grow into a standalone commercial platform without a future migration.

## Vision

- **Titan LifeMap** is the structured AI-guided journey: consumer-first, with downstream routes into Sustain Momentum coaching or IFA referral
- Integrates behavioural finance principles via the Behavioural Friction Profile (diagnostic) — a core Patrick strength, distinct from the separate BEA intervention framework
- Designed to be scalable — a platform model, not a consultancy. Architecture: `sustain-momentum.com` (marketing) → `api.sustain-momentum.com` (GAIA/discovery engine) → `app.sustain-momentum.com` (future client portal, not yet built)
- Connects to the broader theme of closing the UK advice gap in a post-AGBR world
- The **Internal AI Profile** generated by every Titan LifeMap session (never client-visible) is the permanent memory layer GAIA will build on for personalization and returning users — see `context/titan-lifemap/overview.md`

## How GAIA Connects to the Other Ventures

GAIA is the long-term scalable asset in Patrick's portfolio. Where GOIA monetises cyber governance expertise and Sustain Momentum monetises regulatory advisory expertise, GAIA is the platform play — the venture with the largest potential scale. Patrick's AGBR/Targeted Support work creates direct insight into the consumer journey gaps that GAIA could address.
