# GAIA — Overview

> Patrick's AI-powered financial planning platform in development.

---

## What GAIA Is

GAIA is a developing AI-powered financial planning platform designed to help consumers and advisers navigate a structured financial planning process. The long-term vision is a scalable digital planning ecosystem combining financial planning, behavioural support, and AI.

## Stage

Early development / concept phase. The platform is not yet built or commercially deployed. Patrick holds the founding vision and is developing the proposition alongside his work at GOIA and Sustain Momentum.

## Vision

- A structured AI-guided financial planning journey for consumers and/or advisers
- Integrates behavioural finance principles (a core Patrick strength)
- Designed to be scalable — a platform model, not a consultancy
- Connects to the broader theme of closing the UK advice gap in a post-AGBR world

## How GAIA Connects to the Other Ventures

GAIA is the long-term scalable asset in Patrick's portfolio. Where GOIA monetises cyber governance expertise and Sustain Momentum monetises regulatory advisory expertise, GAIA is the platform play — the venture with the largest potential scale. Patrick's AGBR/Targeted Support work creates direct insight into the consumer journey gaps that GAIA could address.
